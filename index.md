# Welcome to Coding Club *Where Innovation Meets Collaboration*


## About Us


We are a community of passionate programmers, developers, and tech enthusiasts dedicated to learning and building amazing projects together. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, you'll find a welcoming home here!

## What We Do


* Weekly coding workshops and tutorials
* Hackathons and coding competitions
* Collaborative projects and open-source contributions
* Tech talks and guest speaker sessions
* Career development and interview preparation
